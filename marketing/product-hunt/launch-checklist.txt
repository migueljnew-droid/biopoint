BIOPOINT — PRODUCT HUNT LAUNCH DAY PLAN
========================================

PRE-LAUNCH (1 Week Before)
--------------------------
[ ] Create Product Hunt maker account (if not already done)
[ ] Upload product: logo (240x240), gallery images (1270x760), GIF or video demo
[ ] Draft and finalize tagline, description, and first comment (this kit)
[ ] Prepare 5+ gallery images: hero shot, blood work analysis screenshot, BioPoint Score, Oracle chat, fasting timer, supplement tracker
[ ] Record a 1-2 min product demo video (Loom or screen recording)
[ ] Line up 10-15 supporters to upvote and leave genuine comments in the first 2 hours
[ ] Schedule social media posts (Twitter, LinkedIn, Instagram) for launch morning
[ ] Notify your email list / community 3 days before: "We're launching on PH Tuesday"
[ ] Prepare answers to expected questions (pricing, data privacy, peptide legality, AI accuracy)
[ ] Test the web app (app.biopointapp.com) — make sure onboarding is smooth for new signups

PRE-LAUNCH (Night Before)
--------------------------
[ ] Schedule the Product Hunt post for 12:01 AM PT (posts go live at midnight Pacific)
[ ] Set alarms for 5:00 AM, 8:00 AM, 12:00 PM, 5:00 PM ET check-ins
[ ] Pre-write thank-you replies for common comment types (praise, feature requests, comparisons)
[ ] Charge your phone — you'll be on it all day

LAUNCH DAY (Hour by Hour)
--------------------------

12:01 AM PT — PRODUCT GOES LIVE
[ ] Verify post is live and displaying correctly
[ ] Post your maker's first comment immediately
[ ] Share PH link on Twitter with personal story angle

5:00 AM ET — FIRST WAVE
[ ] Text/DM your 10-15 core supporters: "We're live! Link: [PH URL]"
[ ] Post on LinkedIn (professional angle — founder journey, building in public)
[ ] Post on Instagram Stories with swipe-up link

8:00 AM ET — MORNING PUSH
[ ] Tweet again with a different angle (feature highlight: AI blood work analysis)
[ ] Reply to every single comment on Product Hunt — be personal, not templated
[ ] Share in relevant communities: biohacking subreddits, peptide forums, longevity Discords
[ ] Post in any Slack/Discord groups you're part of (founder communities, indie hackers)

12:00 PM ET — MIDDAY CHECK
[ ] Post a "halftime update" tweet with current rank and a thank-you
[ ] Reply to all new PH comments
[ ] Share on Instagram feed (carousel post with app screenshots)
[ ] Send email blast to your list: "We're live on Product Hunt — here's the link"

3:00 PM ET — AFTERNOON PUSH
[ ] Tweet a feature spotlight (The Oracle AI chat or BioPoint Score)
[ ] Engage with anyone who tweeted about you — retweet, reply, thank them
[ ] Reply to ALL Product Hunt comments — response rate matters for ranking

5:00 PM ET — FINAL PUSH
[ ] Post final social push: "Last few hours to support us on Product Hunt"
[ ] Thank your supporters publicly on Twitter
[ ] Reply to any remaining PH comments

POST-LAUNCH (Next Day)
-----------------------
[ ] Post results on Twitter/LinkedIn: "We hit #X on Product Hunt! Here's what I learned..."
[ ] Send thank-you email to your list with results
[ ] Compile all feedback from PH comments into a feature request list
[ ] Follow up with anyone who left detailed feedback — potential power users
[ ] Add "Featured on Product Hunt" badge to website and App Store description
[ ] Write a short post-mortem: what worked, what you'd do differently

KEY RULES
---------
- NEVER ask for upvotes directly — say "check us out" or "would love your feedback"
- Respond to EVERY comment — PH rewards engagement
- Be authentic — your story (Grammy nominee turned biohacker) is your edge
- Best launch days: Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday
- Avoid launching on days with major tech events or Apple keynotes
