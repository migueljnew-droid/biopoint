## BioPoint — The Biohacking Command Center

**Stop guessing. Start optimizing.**

BioPoint turns your body into a data-driven system. Track supplements, analyze blood work with AI, and get a daily optimization score — all in one app built by someone who actually lives this lifestyle.

### What You Get

**Supplement Stack Tracker**
Log and manage your daily protocol with access to an 87-compound peptide database. BPC-157, TB-500, GHK-Cu, NAD+ — if serious biohackers use it, it's in here. Set schedules, track dosages, and never miss a stack.

**AI Blood Work Analysis**
Upload a lab report PDF and our AI extracts every biomarker automatically. No manual data entry. Get instant insights on what's optimal, what's flagged, and what to discuss with your doctor.

**BioPoint Score (0-100)**
A single daily number that reflects your optimization state. Pulled from your supplement adherence, biomarker trends, fasting data, and activity. Watch it climb as your protocols dial in.

**The Oracle — AI Health Chat**
Your personal biohacking advisor powered by Gemini. Ask it anything — peptide interactions, stack timing, what your labs mean. It knows your data and gives contextualized answers, not generic wellness fluff.

**Fasting Timer with Zone Tracking**
Built-in intermittent fasting timer that tracks your metabolic zones — fed, early fasted, ketosis, deep autophagy. Know exactly where you are in your fast.

**Community Leaderboards**
Compare BioPoint Scores, share stacks, and learn from other optimizers. See what protocols top performers are running.

### Who It's For

Anyone serious about biological optimization — biohackers, peptide users, longevity enthusiasts, athletes, and quantified-self practitioners who want one place to manage everything.

Built by Miguel Jiminez, a 3x Grammy-nominated artist who went deep into biohacking and couldn't find a single app that handled supplements, blood work, and AI analysis together. So he built one.
