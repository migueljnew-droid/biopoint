## 🏛️ Administrative Safeguards (§164.308)

### §164.308(a)(1) - Security Management Process

#### **Policy: Risk Analysis and Management**
**Policy ID:** ADMIN-001  
**Effective Date:** January 2026  
**Review Frequency:** Annual

##### **Statement:**
BioPoint shall implement comprehensive risk analysis and management procedures to identify, assess, and mitigate risks to the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of ePHI.

##### **Procedures:**

**1. Annual Risk Assessment (§164.308(a)(1)(ii)(A))**
- Conduct comprehensive risk assessment annually
- Document all systems that create, receive, maintain, or transmit ePHI
- Identify potential threats and vulnerabilities
- Assess current security measures and their effectiveness
- Determine risk levels (High/Medium/Low) for each identified risk
- Develop risk mitigation strategies

**Risk Assessment Process:**
```
Step 1: Asset Inventory → Step 2: Threat Identification → Step 3: Vulnerability Assessment
Step 4: Risk Analysis → Step 5: Control Recommendations → Step 6: Documentation
```

**2. Risk Management Implementation (§164.308(a)(1)(ii)(B))**
- Implement security measures to reduce risks to reasonable and appropriate levels
- Prioritize high-risk items for immediate remediation
- Document all implemented security measures
- Regular review and update of security controls
- Quarterly risk management reviews

**3. Sanction Policy (§164.308(a)(1)(ii)(C))**
- Apply sanctions to workforce members who fail to comply with security policies
- Progressive discipline structure:
  - First violation: Written warning and retraining
  - Second violation: Suspension without pay (1-3 days)
  - Third violation: Termination of employment
- Document all violations and sanctions in personnel files
- Report violations to HIPAA Security Officer within 24 hours

**4. Information System Activity Review (§164.308(a)(1)(ii)(D))**
- Review audit logs weekly for unauthorized access attempts
- Monitor system activity for suspicious behavior
- Document all reviews and findings
- Escalate incidents according to incident response procedures

## ⚖️ Enforcement and Sanctions

### Policy Enforcement

#### **Policy: Compliance Enforcement**
**Policy ID:** ENFORCE-001  
**Effective Date:** January 2026

##### **Statement:**
BioPoint enforces compliance with all HIPAA policies and procedures through a graduated sanction system and corrective action process.

##### **Procedures:**
