## RESOLUTION 7
## ESTABLISHMENT OF BANK ACCOUNTS

**RESOLVED**, that the Company is authorized to establish one or more bank accounts at such financial institutions as the Managing Director may select, and that the Managing Director and such other officers as the Managing Director may designate are authorized to:

## RESOLUTION 8
## ESTABLISHMENT OF TRUST ACCOUNTS

**RESOLVED**, that the Company is authorized to establish separate trust accounts for each trust it administers, and that such accounts shall be:

(a) Maintained in the name of the Company as trustee for the applicable trust;

(b) Segregated from the Company's operating accounts;

(c) Subject to the terms of the applicable trust instrument and these Resolutions.

## RESOLUTION 10
## INVESTMENT POLICY

**RESOLVED**, that pending adoption of a comprehensive Investment Policy Statement, the Company shall invest trust assets in accordance with the following principles:

(a) **Prudent Investor Standard.** All investments shall be made in accordance with the Prudent Investor Rule as codified in the Uniform Prudent Investor Act;

(b) **Diversification.** Trust portfolios shall be diversified unless diversification is imprudent under the circumstances or is expressly waived by the trust instrument;

(c) **Trust Terms.** Investments shall be made in accordance with the terms of the applicable trust instrument;

(d) **Risk Assessment.** Investment decisions shall consider the risk and return objectives of each trust and its beneficiaries;

(e) **Costs.** Investment costs shall be reasonable and appropriate.

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that the Managing Director is directed to prepare a comprehensive Investment Policy Statement for approval by the Board within ninety (90) days.

---

## RESOLUTION 11
## FEE SCHEDULE

**RESOLVED**, that pending adoption of a comprehensive fee schedule, the Company shall charge reasonable fees for its fiduciary services, which may include:

| Service | Fee |
|---------|-----|
| Annual Trustee Fee | 0.50% - 1.00% of trust assets |
| Distribution Fee | $50 - $250 per distribution |
| Extraordinary Services | Hourly rate of $150 - $350 |
| Acceptance Fee | $500 - $2,500 per trust |
| Termination Fee | $500 - $2,500 per trust |

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that specific fees for each trust shall be set forth in writing and disclosed to beneficiaries, and that the Managing Director is authorized to negotiate appropriate fees for each trust.

**FURTHER RESOLVED**, that the Managing Director is directed to prepare a comprehensive fee schedule for approval by the Board within sixty (60) days.

## RESOLUTION 16

(c) Significant changes to investment policy;
