### ARTICLE IV
### PURPOSE AND POWERS

**Section 4.01. Purpose.** The Company is organized exclusively for the following purposes:

(a) To act as trustee, co-trustee, agent, or in any other fiduciary capacity for trusts established by or for the benefit of family members related by blood, marriage, or adoption to the organizer(s) and members of the Company, and their respective lineal descendants and spouses thereof;

(b) To act as trustee for charitable trusts and religious organizations established by or affiliated with such family members;

(c) To administer trust estates, manage trust assets, and perform all duties and exercise all powers customarily performed or exercised by corporate trustees;

(d) To hold, manage, invest, and distribute trust property in accordance with the terms of applicable trust instruments and governing law;

(e) To engage in such other lawful activities as are incidental to or necessary for the accomplishment of the foregoing purposes.

**Section 4.02. Private Trust Company Limitation.** The Company shall operate exclusively as a private trust company and shall NOT:

(a) Accept deposits from the public;
(b) Engage in banking or commercial lending activities;
(c) Act as trustee or fiduciary for persons or entities other than those described in Section 4.01;
(d) Solicit trust business from the general public.

**Section 4.03. Specific Trust Authority.** Without limiting the generality of the foregoing, the Company is specifically authorized to serve as trustee for:

(a) OKLA DYNASTY TRUST, a South Dakota perpetual dynasty trust;
(b) Nashoba Tribe of Shabtau Trust;
(c) Jiminez Private Family Foreign Situs Trust;
(d) National Redemption Outreach, a religious assembly;
(e) Such other family trusts as may hereafter be established.

### ARTICLE IX
### LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

**Section 9.01. Manager and Director Liability.** To the fullest extent permitted by the Wyoming Limited Liability Company Act as in effect on the date hereof and as hereafter amended:

(a) No Manager, Director, officer, or employee of the Company shall be personally liable to the Company or its members for monetary damages for breach of fiduciary duty as a Manager, Director, officer, or employee;

(b) No Manager, Director, officer, or employee shall be liable for any act or omission if such person acted in good faith and in a manner such person reasonably believed to be in or not opposed to the best interests of the Company.

**Section 9.02. Exceptions.** The limitation of liability in Section 9.01 shall not eliminate or limit the liability of any person for:

(a) Any breach of the duty of loyalty to the Company or its members;
(b) Acts or omissions not in good faith or which involve intentional misconduct or a knowing violation of law;
(c) Any transaction from which such person derived an improper personal benefit;
(d) Willful or wanton misconduct.

---

### ARTICLE X
### INDEMNIFICATION

**Section 10.01. Indemnification.** The Company shall indemnify any person who was or is a party or is threatened to be made a party to any threatened, pending, or completed action, suit, or proceeding, whether civil, criminal, administrative, or investigative, by reason of the fact that such person is or was a Manager, Director, officer, employee, or agent of the Company, to the fullest extent permitted by the Wyoming Limited Liability Company Act.

**Section 10.02. Advancement of Expenses.** Expenses incurred in defending any action, suit, or proceeding may be paid by the Company in advance of the final disposition of such action, suit, or proceeding upon receipt of an undertaking by or on behalf of such person to repay such amount if it shall ultimately be determined that such person is not entitled to be indemnified by the Company.
